# Prompt Examples

## Cyberpunk Prompt Example

Keywords: cyberpunk, sci-fi, neon, 赛博朋克, 科幻。

Example: Generate a 12 second cinematic electronic cue with dark synth lead, pulsing sub bass, industrial percussion, wide reverb, and neon night ambience. Keep the mood mysterious and driving, with a short motif intro, main groove, contrast variation, and resolved ending.

## Lo-Fi Prompt Example

Keywords: lo-fi, lofi, study, chill, 低保真, 学习。

Example: Generate a 12 second warm lo-fi study loop with mellow keys, soft vinyl texture, gentle percussion, and relaxed bass. Keep the groove stable and unobtrusive, with a calm mood and soft high frequency.

## Pop Hook Prompt Example

Keywords: pop, hook, catchy, 主旋律, 抓耳。

Example: Generate a 12 second pop cue with a foreground lead hook, repeated two-bar motif, clean bass, light percussion, and a clear return of the theme at the ending.

## Ambient Prompt Example

Keywords: ambient, meditation, calm, 氛围, 冥想, 舒缓。

Example: Generate a 12 second ambient texture with slow evolving pads, gentle low bass, long reverb, soft attacks, and spacious calm movement. Avoid dense percussion and keep the sound immersive.

## EDM Prompt Example

Keywords: edm, dance, club, drop, 电子舞曲, 舞曲, 俱乐部, 鼓点。

Example: Generate a 12 second EDM club cue with a punchy kick, sidechain bass, rhythmic synth plucks, rising build-up, and a short but clear drop. Keep the groove stable and energetic.

## Rock Prompt Example

Keywords: rock, band, guitar, drums, 摇滚, 乐队, 吉他, 鼓组。

Example: Generate a 12 second energetic rock cue with an electric guitar riff, bass guitar, live drum kit, and power chord layer. Keep the riff memorable and the rhythm section driving.

## Guofeng Prompt Example

Keywords: guofeng, chinese, traditional, 国风, 古风, 中国风, 古筝, 笛子。

Example: Generate a 12 second Chinese cinematic cue with guzheng plucks, dizi lead, soft strings, deep drum, and a pentatonic motif. Blend traditional timbre with modern cinematic space.

## Sad Piano Prompt Example

Keywords: sad, piano, emotional, 悲伤, 钢琴, 抒情, 治愈。

Example: Generate a 12 second emotional piano cue with intimate piano melody, soft strings, gentle reverb, sparse rhythm, and enough silence between phrases for a reflective mood.
