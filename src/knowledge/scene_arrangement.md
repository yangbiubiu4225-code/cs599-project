# Scene And Arrangement Knowledge

## Night City Scene

Keywords: night city, city night, neon street, urban, 夜景, 城市夜景, 霓虹街道, 都市, 夜晚。

Scene guidance: night city music benefits from pulsing low end, reflective reverb, subtle metallic texture, and a sense of motion. It can lean cyberpunk, synthwave, or cinematic electronic depending on the requested mood.

Arrangement guidance: start with atmosphere or low pulse, introduce a short motif, add percussion gradually, and keep enough reverb tail to suggest space.

## Game Battle Scene

Keywords: game, battle, fight, boss, action, 游戏, 战斗, 打斗, boss, 动作。

Scene guidance: game battle music should communicate urgency, loopability, and clear rhythmic drive. The main motif should survive repetition without becoming tiring.

Arrangement guidance: use strong percussion, short motif repetition, bass ostinato, and a middle variation that can loop back into the main groove.

## Study Background Scene

Keywords: study, work, focus, background, 学习, 工作, 专注, 背景音乐。

Scene guidance: study background music should avoid sudden changes, sharp transients, and overly foreground melodies. Stability and low fatigue matter more than dramatic contrast.

Arrangement guidance: use soft drums, warm chords, mellow bass, gentle noise texture, and a loopable structure.

## Short Film Opening Scene

Keywords: short film, opening, intro, story, 短片, 开场, 片头, 叙事。

Scene guidance: short film openings need fast atmosphere establishment and a clear emotional direction. A compact motif can help the audience remember the scene.

Arrangement guidance: begin with a recognizable texture, introduce a motif within the first few seconds, add a small lift, and end cleanly for editing.

## Healing Emotional Scene

Keywords: healing, emotional, memory, warm, 治愈, 情绪, 回忆, 温柔, 温暖。

Scene guidance: healing emotional music should feel intimate, patient, and human. It often needs soft dynamics, phrase breathing, and a melody that avoids excessive complexity.

Arrangement guidance: use piano, soft pad or strings, gentle reverb, restrained bass, and enough silence between phrases.
