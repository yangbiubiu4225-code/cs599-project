# Music Style Knowledge

## Cyberpunk / Sci-Fi Electronic

Keywords: cyberpunk, sci-fi, futuristic city, neon, night drive, 赛博朋克, 科幻, 霓虹, 城市夜景, 未来感。

Style guidance: cyberpunk music often uses dark synth leads, pulsing sub bass, industrial percussion, wide reverb, delay tails, and cold metallic textures. It works well for city night scenes, neon streets, high-tech suspense, and futuristic openings. Keep the harmony slightly dark, use repeated low-end motifs, and leave space for atmosphere.

Prompt guidance: describe dark synthesizers, sub bass pulse, industrial hits, spacious reverb, neon ambience, and a mysterious but driving mood.

Appreciation guidance: listen for cold timbre, low-frequency movement, metallic transients, spatial depth, and whether the rhythm supports a sense of forward motion.

## Cinematic Trailer / Film Score

Keywords: cinematic, trailer, film score, opening, short film, 影视, 电影, 预告片, 开场, 配乐。

Style guidance: cinematic music should quickly establish scene, mood, and narrative direction. It often uses a clear motif, gradual layering, contrast between intro and main section, and a resolved ending. Electronic cinematic music can combine synth pads, bass pulses, percussion impacts, and a lead motif.

Prompt guidance: mention scene-building intro, memorable motif, evolving layers, section contrast, and clean ending.

Appreciation guidance: evaluate whether the music establishes atmosphere quickly, whether the motif is recognizable, and whether the structure supports visual storytelling.

## Lo-Fi / Study Loop

Keywords: lo-fi, lofi, chill, study, cozy, warm, 低保真, 学习, 放松, 温暖。

Style guidance: lo-fi music usually favors warm texture, soft drums, gentle swing, tape noise, mellow chords, and a stable loop structure. It should avoid overly sharp transients and leave enough space for concentration.

Prompt guidance: describe warm keys, soft vinyl texture, gentle percussion, mellow bass, and relaxed loop structure.

Appreciation guidance: listen for warmth, steadiness, low fatigue, soft high frequency, and whether the groove remains unobtrusive.

## Pop Hook / Catchy Melody

Keywords: pop, hook, catchy, memorable, radio, 主旋律, 抓耳, 流行, 记忆点。

Style guidance: pop-oriented music needs a memorable two-bar or four-bar hook, clear repetition, foreground lead melody, and supportive accompaniment. The rhythm should leave room for the hook instead of masking it.

Prompt guidance: ask for a foreground lead hook, repeated melodic motif, clean accompaniment, and a strong return of the theme near the ending.

Appreciation guidance: evaluate whether a listener can remember the main motif after one pass and whether the arrangement supports the melody.

## Ambient / Meditation

Keywords: ambient, meditation, calm, peaceful, space, drone, 氛围, 冥想, 平静, 空间, 舒缓。

Style guidance: ambient music emphasizes texture, slow evolution, long reverb, soft attacks, and a sense of spaciousness. It should avoid dense percussion unless the user asks for rhythm.

Prompt guidance: describe slow pads, gentle evolving textures, long reverb, low transient density, and calm emotional flow.

Appreciation guidance: listen for smooth transitions, low onset activity, balanced low energy, and whether the sound stays immersive without becoming empty.
